Studio overview

Short description: PasarGad Studio is an indie game studio creating story-driven experiences inspired by the ruins and legends of Pasargad in Persia.

Long description: PasarGad Studio is a small dreamer team in love with creativity, atmosphere, and the quiet edges of storytelling. Our games blend mystery, history, and philosophy, inviting players to wander through ancient spaces, listen to forgotten voices, and make choices that reveal what humanity truly values. Each project is built as a carefully crafted journey rather than a quick distraction.
